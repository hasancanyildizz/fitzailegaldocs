## 2. Description of Service

FocusFlow is a Pomodoro timer application designed to help users:
- Manage focused work sessions using the Pomodoro Technique
- Track tasks and associate them with focus sessions
- Take structured short and long breaks
- View productivity statistics
- Customize timer durations and preferences

## 3. The Pomodoro Technique

FocusFlow implements the Pomodoro Technique, a time management method. The default settings are:
- 25-minute focus sessions
- 5-minute short breaks
- 15-minute long breaks (after every 4 pomodoros)

These settings are customizable within the app.
