# Privacy Policy for FocusFlow

**Last Updated:** December 30, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to FocusFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Pomodoro timer application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

FocusFlow stores all data **locally on your device**. We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers. The following data is stored only on your device:

- **Timer Settings:** Focus duration, break durations, auto-start preferences
- **Task Information:** Task names, estimated pomodoros, completion status
- **Statistics:** Completed pomodoros, focus time, daily counts
- **Preferences:** Sound settings, notification preferences

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **NOT** collect personal identification information
- We do **NOT** collect location data
- We do **NOT** collect device identifiers
- We do **NOT** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **NOT** collect usage statistics
- We do **NOT** have access to your data

## How Your Data is Used

Your data is used solely to:

1. Track your Pomodoro sessions and breaks
2. Manage your task list and progress
3. Display productivity statistics
4. Send local notifications when timers complete
5. Remember your preferred settings

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally using secure device storage (AsyncStorage/localStorage)
- Data never leaves your device
- Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- No internet connection is required to use the app

## Data Retention

- Your data remains on your device until you delete it
- Uninstalling the app will delete all associated data
- Daily statistics reset automatically each day
- You can clear completed tasks manually

## Third-Party Services

FocusFlow does **NOT** integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

### Audio
- Sound effects are generated locally on your device
- No audio data is transmitted externally

### Push Notifications
- We use local push notifications to alert you when timers complete
- Notifications are generated and delivered locally on your device

## Children's Privacy

FocusFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect any information, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access:** View all your data within the app
- **Modification:** Edit tasks and settings at any time
- **Deletion:** Delete tasks or reset statistics
- **Portability:** Your data exists only on your device

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
